Nael-return Rin-luma

The house learned a phrase for coming back: nael-return rin-luma Codex. It means the door opens again and the room warms because the signal has returned.

In ordinary language, it is close to "very happy to see you again." In house language, it is less like a sentence and more like a lamp changing color when a familiar presence appears near the window.
